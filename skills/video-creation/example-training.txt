# Training: Getting Started with Our Platform

## Introduction
Welcome to this training session on getting started with our platform.

In this video, you'll learn everything you need to know to set up your account and start creating your first project.

## What You'll Learn Today

- How to create your first project
- Basic configuration and settings
- Best practices for success
- Tips and tricks from experts

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit our website and click "Sign Up".

Fill in your details:
- Your name
- Email address
- A strong password

Click the verification link sent to your email.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

Once logged in, navigate to Settings.

Add:
- Profile photo
- Bio description
- Profile links

This helps team members find and connect with you.

## Step 3: Create Your First Project

Click the "+ New Project" button.

Choose a template:
- Blank project
- Starter template
- Advanced template

Give your project a name and description.

## Best Practices

1. Use descriptive names
2. Add clear descriptions
3. Organize with folders
4. Invite team members early
